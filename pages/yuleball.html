Event Overview

In the magical Wadley Farms Castle, wizarding dreams came true for all. Here's how the evening went:

  • Guests entered the castle and recieved their magical maps
  • Sorting Ceremony in the Great Hall
  • Photo Opps with live owls and live snakes
  • Guests explore the castle to see the potion dungeon, chamber of secrets, gryffindor common room, divination loft, and owl loft.
  • Attended a "magic class" (watch magic performed by a live magician)

Here's more of what guests experienced at the Yule Ball:

  • Learned their fortune in the Divination Loft
  • Played a game of Giant Chess
  • Drank a potion from the soda bar
  • Danced all night
  • Represented their house during the dance battle
